The beginning of the way

Alexander Mikhailovich Ovechkin (born September 17, 1985, Moscow) is a Russian hockey player, left wing and captain of the Washington Capitals NHL club. Stanley Cup Winner 2018. Three-time world champion (2008, 2012, 2014). He began his professional career at Dynamo Moscow at the age of 16. Was selected by Washington in the 2004 draft, becoming the second Russian hockey player after Ilya Kovalchuk, selected under the overall first number [6]. In the American club he spent 15 seasons, in 2010 he became the team captain. In January 2008, he signed a 13-year contract with Washington for $ 124 million [7], thus becoming the first hockey player to sign a contract for more than $ 100 million. He made his debut in the NHL in the 2005/06 season, receiving the prize for the best newcomer - the Calder Trophy. Nine times won the prize for the best sniper - "Maurice Richard Trophy", three times received prizes for the most valuable player - "Ted Lindsay Eward" [8] and "Hart Trophy" and once the prize for the best scorer of the season - "Art Ross Trophy".

Personal life

Personal life

In 2015, the hockey player began dating 21-year-old model Anastasia Shubskaya, the daughter of actress Vera Glagoleva and millionaire Kirill Shubsky. They met at the Beijing Olympics (2008), but did not maintain a relationship for a long time. In 2015, the girl almost drowned in Dubai and told about the incident to her Instagram subscribers. Among them was Ovechkin. Concerned about Nastya's condition, he decided to support her personally. So their whirlwind romance began, which ended with a magnificent wedding. The celebration took place in July 2017. In 2018, Ovechkin received two major gifts from fate: his team won the Stanley Cup, and his young wife Anastasia became pregnant. On August 18, their son was born, the first child of both Ovi and Shubskaya.
